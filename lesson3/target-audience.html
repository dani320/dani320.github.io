Audience

The target audience are those that want to know the weather so that they know what events they can plan for ahead of time. This is great for those who also intend on traveling to other parts of the country or other countries within the next couple of days.

First scenario

Traveling to Puerto Rico and wants to know if it will rain a lot or if it will be sunny everyday.

Second scenario

Is traveling to Italy and wants to know what kind of clothes they should pack for because they have no idea what the weather is like over there.

Third scenario

The kids have soccer practice and they need to know if it will be raining and cold to so that they can provide them with the proper clothes.

Fourth scenario

It is summer and they need to know if they will be able to go swimming in the pool then they need to know that it will not be lighting.

Fifth scenario

The school district will need to know how the weather is so that they can inform parents if it is to icy and heavy snowing before the children need to go to school.
